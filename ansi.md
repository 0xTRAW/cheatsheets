Format

    \033[#m

Where:

    0      clear
    1      bold
    4      underline
    5      blink

    30-37  fg color
    40-47  bg color

    1K     clear line (to beginning of line)
    2K     clear line (entire line)
    2J     clear screen
    0;0H   move cursor to 0;0

Colors

    0      black
    1      red
    2      green
    3      yellow
    4      blue
    5      magenta
    6      cyan
    7      white
